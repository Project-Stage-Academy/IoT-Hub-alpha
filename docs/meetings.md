# Team Meetings and Sprint Cadence

This document defines the recurring meetings, synchronization points, and communication
cadence for the IoT Catalog Hub internship.

The internship cohort consists of two parallel teams working independently on the same
project scope. Each team follows the same governance model, sprint structure, and quality
standards.

---

## 1. Sprint Structure

- **Sprint length:** 2 weeks
- **Sprint goal:** Deliver a set of User Stories that meet the Definition of Done (DoD)
- **Evaluation:** Weekly formative feedback with milestone-based grading

---

## 2. Team-Level Meetings (Per Team)

Each team consists of approximately 7 interns and operates independently.

| Meeting | Day | Time (EET) | Duration | Participants |
|------|----|----|----|----|
| **Team Daily Sync** | Mon – Fri | 12:00 | ~20 min | Team |
| **Sprint Planning** | Weekly | 12:00 | ~30 min | Team |
| **Team Retrospective (optional)** | Weekly | TBD | ~20 min | Team |

### Team Daily Sync
**Purpose:**
- Align on daily tasks and priorities
- Identify blockers and dependencies
- Coordinate work within the team

**Format:**
- What was done since the last sync?
- What is planned next?
- Are there any blockers?

**Owner:** Rotating facilitator (team member)

---

### Sprint Planning
**Purpose:**
- Select User Stories from the backlog
- Break stories into tasks
- Agree on sprint scope and priorities

**Outcome:**
- Committed sprint backlog for the team

**Owner:** Project Owner or designated team representative

---

## 3. All-Hands Meetings (Entire Cohort)

All interns (approximately 13 people) participate in shared sessions led by mentors.

| Meeting | Day | Time (EET) | Duration | Participants |
|------|----|----|----|----|
| **All-Hands Sessions** | Mon – Fri | 17:00 – 19:00 | up to 2h | Full Team |
| **Weekly Demo** | Weekly | During all-hands | ~30 min | Interns + Mentors |

### All-Hands Sessions
**Purpose:**
- Technical lectures and workshops
- Cross-team alignment
- Mentoring and Q&A sessions
- Progress reviews and guidance

**Owner:** Mentors / Program Coordinators

---

### Weekly Demo
**Purpose:**
- Demonstrate working features in a live environment (e.g. Docker Compose)
- Receive mentor feedback
- Share implementation approaches between teams

**Owner:** Mentors / Program Coordinators

---

## 4. Communication Channels

- **Primary Chat:** Discord channel `#iot-hub-alpha`
- **Daily Coordination:** Team-specific Discord threads or channels
- **Code & Design Discussions:** GitHub Issues and Pull Requests
- **Urgent Blockers:** Mention mentors or use agreed escalation tags in Discord
- **Video Calls:** Discord / Zoom / Google Meet  
  *(links are shared privately via the primary chat and are not published in the repository)*

---

## 5. Notes

- One person may facilitate or own multiple meetings depending on team needs.
- Meeting times may be adjusted with prior agreement from mentors and the team.
- Transparency and regular communication are expected from all participants.
